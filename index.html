About Me

Hello! Welcome to my little corner of the web. My name is Janeé Mays. I grew up on a small farm in northeastern Ohio. Our family farm was nested in a small town on the fringe of the "yinzer" rust belt, along the Ohio River in an old forgotten pottery town. Now I live in Columbus, Ohio with my husband Luke, and two rescue dogs, Pippi and Pooka. When I'm not working I like to get outside and explore new places and experience new things. I also enjoy traveling and reading a good book.

I began my career in operations and quickly found a niche among exact calculations. After spending ten years in the financial industry, I decided to spread my wings and learn to code! I love the creativity and versatility that web development nurtures.

Do you have a design you'd like to execute but you're not sure where to start? Reach out to me, I'd love to hear about it. Allow me to help develop the foundation so you can concentrate on the overall success of your project. I'm confident that we can work together to create something uniquely qualified to meet your needs, whatever they might be. Thanks for stopping by! I look forward to working together.
